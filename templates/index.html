Got questions

We Got Answers.

Do I need to upload my VOD or stream link?+
No. PogClip's Live Engine runs quietly alongside your stream the moment you go live. There is nothing to upload. Your clips are captured and processed in real time, automatically.
How does PogClip know when to clip?+
PogClip watches your live chat, your audio, and your screen in real time. When messages spike, your voice gets excited, or something big happens on screen — it scores the excitement level and triggers a clip automatically.
Does it work with Twitch and Kick?+
Yes. Both platforms are supported. Kick support is available on Creator and Pro plans.
Will this slow down my stream or PC?+
Not at all. PogClip's Live Engine is built to be lightweight. It runs as a background process that doesn't compete with your stream for resources. Your stream quality stays exactly the same.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard in two clicks.
